Current Information

Amazon is most known for being one of the largest online marketplaces. The current CEO of Amazon is Jeff Bezos.

Current Services

E-Commerce

Amazon is an online marketplace that sells a variety of products, ranging from groceries, to clothing, to appliances.

Amazon Web Services

Amazon provides Amazon Web Services, which is a cloud-computing platform. This is used by many companies who have a large online presesnce.

Telehealth

Amazon provides One Medical, a telehealth service that connects doctors with patients.

History

Amazon was founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos. In the beginning, Amazon's main product was books, but it slowly transitioned to selling more items, becoming the marketplace that it is today.
